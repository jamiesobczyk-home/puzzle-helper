Puzzle Piece Finder

Photograph the box, scan your pieces, and get told where they go.

1 The picture on the box

Take a photo of the box art (or the example page from inside the box). Then drag on the image to crop it down to just the puzzle picture — tight crops give better matches.

Drag a rectangle over the puzzle picture to crop. Current crop is highlighted.

2 Your puzzle pieces

Lay one or more pieces picture-side up on a plain background (a sheet of paper works well) and take a photo. You can add several photos.

3 Where do they go?

Everything runs in your browser — no photos leave your device.
